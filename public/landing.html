Autism

Autism spectrum disorder (ASD), refers to a range of conditions characterized by challenges with social skills, repetitive behaviors, speech and nonverbal communication. The term 'spectrum' reflects the wide variation in challenges and strengths possessed by each person with autism.


Alexithymia

A prevalent constituent of the ASD, Alexithymia is a personality construct that reflects the inability to recogonise or describe emotions. Lack of emotional awareness combined with difficulty in distinguishing and appreciating emotions of others often leads to false impressions of apathy and concern.


ASCEND

Our product provides a real-time interface to aid autistic people in their day-to-day communications via speech. The algorithm identifies emotion from audio irrespective of language and offers a suggestive reaction. Concerned doctors and teachers can also monitor with a background 24/7 reporting facility.

How ASCEND works

01

Sign up

Download the ASCEND app from the Android Play Store, sign up, and make your account to begin your journey with us!


02

Record

You can record in real-time with instantaneous results or statically to access the recordings later. The app shows the emotion detected along with a suggested reaction.


03

Analyse

Visit the dashboard to view the analysis at a glance. Useful for doctors and teachers alike, the dashboard has it all!
